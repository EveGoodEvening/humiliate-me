# Source summary

This skill is based on the user's Chinese source material about asking AI to “泼冷水” rather than asking “我这想法怎么样”. The core principle is to ask “我这想法最可能在哪里崩” and keep pressure-testing until the weak points stop moving.

## Source patterns to preserve

- Before coding a requirement, ask the AI to stop and use Socratic questioning to clarify the requirement first.
- After a technical plan exists, ask for hidden assumptions, overengineering, and an extremely unfriendly review with no emotional comfort.
- For product and pricing decisions, roleplay a biased investor who just heard a competitor's pitch and names the three strongest reasons not to invest.
- For market entry, examine products that died in the last five years, group their failure modes, and identify where the user's plan resembles them.
- For major personal decisions, write from the perspective of a future regretful self and name concrete losses and self-deceptions.

## Safety adaptation

The skill name and likely trigger phrases include “humiliate me” / “羞辱我”, but the intended useful behavior is adversarial critique, not personal degradation. The skill should attack ideas, plans, assumptions, evidence, incentives, and decision logic. It should not attack identity, worth, protected characteristics, body, trauma, mental health, or other personal attributes.
